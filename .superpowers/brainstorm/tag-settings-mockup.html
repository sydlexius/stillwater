Matching & Normalization
How tag values are matched and deduplicated across providers.
Canonical spelling normalization
Normalize variations (e.g. "Synth-Pop", "synthpop", "Synth Pop") to a single canonical form. Uses a curated dictionary seeded from AllMusic's taxonomy.
Case-insensitive deduplication
Treat "Alternative Rock" and "alternative rock" as the same value, keeping the canonical casing.
